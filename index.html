Customized bootstrap navbars

Sets of customized navbars with maintaining bootstrap's main code structure. Simple and minimal modifications to demonstrate the possibilities and different ways of customization.

Although you can copy the sass and use these navbars right away, I recommend to check the codes and adapt them for your workflow.
